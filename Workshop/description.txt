[h1]School Buses[/h1]

Turn the cosmetic yellow school bus into a real school bus service: only enrolled students ride it, and a school's routes are generated, and kept up to date, with a single click.

[h1]What it does[/h1]

In vanilla Cities: Skylines the school bus is just a skin, so anyone boards it. This mod makes school bus lines actually carry students.

[b]Students only.[/b] On a line you mark as a school line, only children and teenagers enrolled at that school may board. Adults, tourists and other schools' students are turned away and find another route.

[b]Invisible to everyone else.[/b] Non-students do not even consider a school line when planning a trip, so they never crowd your school stops. Works with the vanilla pathfinder and with TM:PE.

[b]No depot needed.[/b] The school is the depot: generated routes get their bus from the school itself. It spawns there and parks back there, like a real school bus.

[b]Free for students.[/b] School transport is a school service, not paid transit: no fares and no transit maintenance on school lines (you can turn this off if you prefer paid lines).

[b]One click per school.[/b] Click a school or high school and press Generate Routes. The mod reads the current student roster and lays out tidy bus routes covering where the students actually live, splitting a big school into several short routes (one bus each), just like a real district.

[b]One click for the whole city.[/b] Prefer to do it all at once? Generate routes for every school in the city from the mod options (with a heads-up about how many lines and buses that means).

[b]Routes that keep themselves current.[/b] Students grow up and families move. The mod watches each school's coverage and automatically regenerates routes that have drifted out of date, so stops keep following the students. Don't want that? Turn it off and manage lines by hand.

[b]Sensible by default, tunable if you want.[/b] The defaults were tuned in-game for good coverage and ridership across school sizes (and work with custom modded schools of any size). Sliders let you trade coverage against bus count, change route length, and more, with a one-click 'restore recommended defaults'.

[h1]Quick start: make a bus line students-only[/h1]

This is the basic behaviour. Turn any existing bus line into a school line:

[olist]
[*]Open the bus line's info panel.
[*]Tick [b]School line[/b].
[/olist]

The mod finds the [b]nearest school automatically[/b] and binds the line to it (that stop becomes the school stop). From then on, only that school's students may board, riding between home and school; everyone else finds another route (non-students stop planning trips with this line at all). Untick to make it a normal line again.

Note: this only changes [i]who[/i] may board. It does not change the line's vehicles or how many buses it runs, so set those yourself if you want. (The yellow one-bus-per-route school styling is applied to routes the mod generates for you, below.)

[h1]Quick start: auto-generate a school's routes[/h1]

[olist]
[*]Subscribe to and enable this mod and [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2040656402]Harmony[/url], then load your city.
[*]Click an [b]elementary school or high school[/b]. A panel opens on the side. No bus depot needed: the school spawns its own buses.
[*]Press [b]Generate Routes[/b]. The mod reads the school's students and creates one or more school bus routes covering where they live. Done.
[/olist]

[b]Route the whole city at once:[/b] mod options, [b]City-wide routes[/b], [b]Generate routes for all schools[/b].

[i]Tip: as your city grows and families move, routes regenerate themselves to keep coverage up. You can turn that off in the options.[/i]

[h1]Compatibility[/h1]

[list]
[*]TM:PE: fully supported, including its pathfinder and Parking AI (non-students stay off school lines there too).
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=424106600]Improved Public Transport Essentials[/url]: pairs cleanly (per-line vehicles, costs; school buses correctly show no depot cost).
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3721119397]Impatient Commuters[/url]: pairs cleanly; students waiting for their school bus won't give up.
[*]UI Resolution: panels stay on screen at any UI scale.
[*]Designed to be non-destructive and to layer on top of vanilla; every patch is scoped to school lines or falls back to vanilla. No known conflicts with other transit mods.
[/list]
