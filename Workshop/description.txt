[h1]School Buses[/h1]

Turn the cosmetic yellow school bus into a real school bus service: only enrolled students ride it, and a school's routes are generated, and kept up to date, with a single click.

[h1]What it does[/h1]

In vanilla Cities: Skylines the school bus is just a skin, so anyone boards it. This mod makes school bus lines actually carry students.

[b]Students only.[/b] On a line you mark as a school line, only children and teenagers enrolled at that school may board. Adults, tourists and other schools' students are turned away and find another route.

[b]Like a real school run.[/b] Students board at any neighbourhood stop on the way to school, and only at the school itself on the way home.

[b]One click per school.[/b] Click a school or high school and press Generate Routes. The mod reads the current student roster and lays out tidy bus routes covering where the students actually live, splitting a big school into several short routes (one bus each), just like a real district.

[b]One click for the whole city.[/b] Prefer to do it all at once? Generate routes for every school in the city from the mod options (with a heads-up about how many lines and buses that means).

[b]Routes that keep themselves current.[/b] Students grow up and families move. The mod watches each school's coverage and automatically regenerates routes that have drifted out of date, so stops keep following the students. Don't want that? Turn it off and manage lines by hand.

[b]Sensible by default, tunable if you want.[/b] The defaults were tuned in-game for good coverage and ridership across school sizes (and work with custom modded schools of any size). Sliders let you trade coverage against bus count, change route length, and more, with a one-click "restore recommended defaults".

[h1]How to use[/h1]

[list]
[*]Subscribe and enable this mod plus [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2040656402]Harmony[/url].
[*]Click an elementary school or high school and press [b]Generate Routes[/b].
[*]Or route the whole city from the mod options under [b]City-wide routes[/b].
[*]Make sure you have a bus depot (or a few) so the buses can spawn. Each route runs one bus.
[*]You can also mark any existing bus line as a school line in its info panel.
[/list]

[h1]Compatibility[/h1]

[list]
[*]Improved Public Transport 2: used (when present) to pin exactly one bus per route. Recommended.
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3721119397]Impatient Commuters[/url]: pairs cleanly; students waiting for their school bus won't give up.
[*]Designed to be non-destructive and to layer on top of vanilla; no known conflicts with other transit mods.
[/list]
